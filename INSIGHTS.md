# SQL Query Insights

**Task 1:** A small number of "Top 5" customers drive a disproportionate amount of revenue, highlighting a critical need to retain these VIPs.

**Task 2:** "Revenue by Category" identifies our core products. Comparing this to the "Delivered" variant reveals potential fulfillment bottlenecks in categories with a large revenue gap.

**Task 3:** The "Employees Above Average" report is an HR tool to spot top performers and identify "flight risks" (high-performers in low-paying departments).

**Task 4:** "Gold" customer counts show geographic loyalty hotspots. These cities are prime targets for new product launches or referral programs.

**Task 4 Ext:** The full loyalty breakdown identifies cities with many "Bronze/Silver" members, revealing perfect audiences for "level-up" marketing campaigns.
